About WanCloud

Wanclouds is a startup headquartered in Santa Clara, California with remote offices in Bangalore and Islamabad. We focus on providing operational and technical support for Openstack clouds as well as assist with software development activities on various front-end and back-end technologies. Our team has diverse and in depth experience in cloud, networking and various open source technologies such as Openstack, Dockers, and open networking. Our team has the unique advantage and experience when it comes to operating and supporting a global multi-site cloud using Openstack and other open source technologies. Our team members come from Cisco, VMware, SAP, and various other companies with primary backgrounds in cloud and open source technologies.
